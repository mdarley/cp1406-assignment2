Blair Harris

Info about Blair Harris

Born and bred in New Zealand, Blair Harris began his cello studies under the guidance of Gary Wilby and Dr Rolf Gjelsten. Blair’s passion for music quickly took hold and led him to win the Top Scholar Award for Music in the National Qualifications Examinations of New Zealand.

Currently a member of the Melbourne Chamber Orchestra, Blair has held numerous guest orchestral roles, including a principal position with Orchestra Victoria and associate principal of the Tasmanian Symphony.

As an avid devotee of new music, Blair is a member of the contemporary chamber music group Syzygy Ensemble. Currently an ensemble in residence at the Melbourne Recital Centre, Syzygy is known for dynamic performances of modern chamber music repertoire. In 2012 Syzygy were nominated for a Helpmann Award in the ‘Best Chamber Music Performance’ category.
